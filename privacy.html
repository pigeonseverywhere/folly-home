privacy policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

folly is a shared budget tracker for two people. It does not collect your data. QED.

For the longer version, here's what you should know about your data:

where your data lives

All expense entries, your name, and your email address are stored locally on your device. When you settle expenses, your entries are synced to a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive account, a sheet you own and control. Your budget partner can read this sheet — I can't.

third parties

folly uses Google Sign-In for authentication and the Google Sheets and Drive APIs to sync settlements to your own Google Drive. I used this so I don't have to touch your data (or host a server). Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

what I don't do

I do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data. I do not track you, sell your data, or use any advertising or analytics services.

data deletion

Leaving a budget you've joined or deleting a budget you own wipes all local data from your device. If you created the budget, folly also deletes the shared Google Sheet from your Drive. If you joined a budget, leaving only clears your local data — the sheet sticks around in the owner's Google Drive until they delete the budget.

changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
