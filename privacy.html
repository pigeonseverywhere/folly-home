privacy policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

folly is a shared budget tracker for two people. It does not collect your data. QED.

For the longer version, here's what you should know about your data:

where your data lives

All expense entries, your name, and your email address are stored locally on your device. When you settle expenses, your entries are synced to a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive account, a sheet you own and control. Your budget partner can read this sheet — we can't.

third parties

folly uses Google Sign-In for authentication and the Google Sheets and Drive APIs to sync settlements to your own Google Drive. We used this so we don't have to touch your data (or host a server). Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

what we don't do

We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data. We do not track you, sell your data, or use any advertising or analytics services.

data deletion

Leaving or deleting your budget wipes all local data from your device. Your Google Sheet sticks around in your Google Drive until you delete it manually.

changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
